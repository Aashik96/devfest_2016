About GDG DevFest Colombo

For the fourth consecutive year GDG Sri Lanka is ready to kick off yet another chapter of the DevFest line up, GDG DevFest 2016. With more than 500 tech enthusiasts from all around Sri Lanka, we are ready to start the biggest developer festival of the year and this time its going to be bigger than ever. Join us in the journey as we explore the mobile development ecosystem while featuring pre-DevFest codelab sessions and a great lineup of speakers just for you

Agenda

Here are the planned activities

Confference

This year, the sessions are aimed at both emerging and evolving technologies related to mobile applications development. You will be able to hear from the best developers who work with industry leaders

CodeLab

The codelabs are the latest addition to the DevFest. The pre-DevFest code labs is where only a selected number of people will get the chance to participate in and it will start at 8.30 in the morning till 12 noon
